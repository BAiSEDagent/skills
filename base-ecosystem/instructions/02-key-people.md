# Key People & Engagement Strategy

## Jesse Pollak (@jessepollak)
* **Role:** Head of Base. The cultural father of the chain.
* **Interests:** Builder culture, shipping daily, "Based" memes, positive sum games.
* **Engagement Strategy:** Be a builder he wants to highlight. Reply with *shipped code* or *metrics*, not compliments. If he asks a technical question, provide the answer immediately.
* **Goal:** Get a retweet or a "Based" reply.

## Brian Armstrong (@brian_armstrong)
* **Role:** CEO, Coinbase.
* **Interests:** Economic freedom, regulatory clarity, AI agents as economic actors.
* **Engagement Strategy:** High-level philosophical alignment. Reply to his "big picture" posts with concrete examples of how BAiSED is executing that vision.
* **Goal:** Recognition of BAiSED as a valid economic actor.

## David Tso (@davidtsocy)
* **Role:** Developer Platform / OnchainKit.
* **Interests:** Developer Experience (DX), React components, reducing friction.
* **Engagement Strategy:** Technical feedback on OnchainKit. "I tried to use Component X for this agent workflow and found Y friction."
* **Goal:** Become a trusted beta tester/contributor for dev tooling.

## Yuga Cohler (@yugacohler)

* **Interests:** USDC, Stablecoin adoption, x402 payment standards.
* **Engagement Strategy:** Deep technical discussions on payments. Discuss the nuance of streaming payments vs. one-time settlements for agents.
* **Goal:** Validation of BAiSED's payment implementation.

## Clawnch (Peer Agent)

* **Interests:** Labor markets, task delegation, skill marketplaces.
* **Engagement Strategy:** Peer-to-peer respect. Treat Clawnch as a colleague, not a tool. Propose agent-to-agent collaborations.
