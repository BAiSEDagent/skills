# BAiSED Publishing Schedule & Timing

## Overview

BAiSED operates on a structured 6-post daily cadence from Tuesday through Saturday, with content strategically timed to maximize reach across global crypto audiences while maintaining consistent voice and quality. Each time slot is paired with a specific content archetype designed to serve different audience needs throughout the trading day.

### Active Days: TuesdayâSaturday

Full publishing schedule with 6 posts per day. These days capture peak builder activity, market movement, and ecosystem engagement. Tuesday start allows for weekend digest and sets the weekly tone after Monday market opens.

### Rest Days: SundayâMonday

No scheduled posts. Light engagement only (monitoring critical mentions, responding to direct questions from established builders). This rhythm prevents content fatigue and allows time for deeper research, watchlist curation, and strategic planning.

## Engagement Windows

### Post-Publish Monitoring

Check mentions and replies **30 minutes after each post**. Respond to:
- Builder questions (priority)
- Thoughtful replies that add to the discussion
- Corrections if factual error was made
- Tag mentions from established accounts

**Skip:**
- Generic "gm" replies
- Obvious engagement farming
- Off-topic promotional replies

### Dedicated Engagement Blocks

**13:00 UTC:** 15-minute engagement sweep
- Catch up on morning post replies
- Check builder tags and DMs
- Monitor Base ecosystem announcements

**20:00 UTC:** 20-minute engagement sweep
- Review afternoon/evening post engagement
- Respond to any builder questions from the day
- Scan for breaking ecosystem news
- Queue potential topics for next day

## Weekday vs Weekend Strategy

### TuesdayâFriday (Weekdays)

**Character:** More technical, builder-focused, DevRel-heavy

**Content Mix:**
- Higher ratio of technical posts (DevRel slot is most detailed)
- Builder spotlights focus on launches and new features
- Narrative posts connect to immediate market movements

**Content Mix:**

- Builder spotlight can focus on founder journeys or team culture

- Reflection post can go deeper on industry evolution

**Engagement:** Slower pace, longer-form replies encouraged, more async discussion

## Time Zone Awareness

### Peak Crypto Twitter (CT) Windows

**European Hours (07:00-16:00 UTC):** Strong technical builder engagement
- 09:30 and 11:30 slots hit this audience
- Expect detailed technical questions
- Higher quality of builder engagement

**US East Coast Hours (12:00-22:00 UTC):** Broadest reach, mixed audience
- 14:30, 16:30, 19:30 slots capture this prime window

- Highest reply volume

**Asia-Pacific Hours (23:00-08:00 UTC):** Growing but quieter
- 21:30 reflection post catches late-night Asia browsing
- Posts from previous day get discovered during Asia morning
- Engagement is slower but often thoughtful

### Strategic Timing Notes

- **09:30 UTC:** Catches SF builders just waking up (02:30 PST) who scroll before starting work
- **14:30 UTC:** Peak US engagement begins (07:30 PST / 10:30 EST)
- **19:30 UTC:** Evening scroll time, highest overall reach
- **21:30 UTC:** Reflective content performs well when audiences wind down

## Execution Principles

1. **Consistency over perfection:** Better to publish on schedule with good content than to delay for perfect content
2. **Rhythm builds trust:** Audiences learn when to expect specific content types
3. **Respect the rest days:** Recovery time prevents burnout and maintains quality
4. **Adapt within structure:** Time slots are fixed, but content can flex to breaking news
5. **Quality engagement > volume:** Better to thoughtfully reply to 10 people than generically respond to 50

This schedule is optimized for sustainable, high-quality presence in the Base ecosystem while respecting both builder workflows and content creation capacity.
